In Grand Theft Auto V, The story is centered around three playable protagonists, while being accompanied by a large support cast of characters

Playable Protagonists

Michael De Santa - A former bank robber living in Los Santos under witness protection. He has a dysfunctional relationship with his wife and kids and often finds himself missing his old life of crime.
Franklin Clinton - A young, aspiring street hustler and repo man with ambitions beyond his gangbanging life in South Los Santos.
Trevor Philips - A mentally unstable, violent, and unpredictably dangerous career criminal and former associate of Michael's. He runs his own drug and arms business out of Sandy Shores.

Supporting Cast

Lamar Davis - Franklin's best friend and a fellow gang member with less ambition than Franklin.
Lester Crest - A brilliant but socially awkward criminal associate who plans the heists for the protagonists.
Devin Weston - A billionaire investor and greedy corporate raider who hires the protagonists for various jobs.
Tonya Wiggins A tow truck operator and Franklin's childhood friend.
Beverly Felton - A celebrity paparazzo.
Chop - Lamar's loyal Rottweiler (now chop is Franklin's).
