Features of Grand Theft Auto V

One of the most notable features of GTA V is its vast open world, which is filled with a variety of activities and locations to explore. Players can engage in everything from street racing and skydiving to scuba diving and hunting. The game also features a dynamic weather system and day-night cycle, which adds to the immersive experience.

Another key feature of GTA V is its multiplayer mode, known as Grand Theft Auto Online. This mode allows players to create their own characters and participate in a variety of activities with other players. Players can also purchase properties, vehicles, and weapons (while also being able to customize these items) and can even form their own criminal organizations!!

Fun Fact~ Did you know that GTA V is one of the best-selling video games of all time, with over 150 million copies sold worldwide as of 2021!!!

The Online mode has also been continuously updated with new content and features since its release, which is why it's still so popular today!!!
