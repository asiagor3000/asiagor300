Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was released in September 2013 for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360, followed by releases for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in November 2014, and for Microsoft Windows in April 2015. The game is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which is based on Southern California, and features a sprawling open world that players can explore freely.
